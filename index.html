Welcome to TIMBERWORKS CONSTRUCTION

Timberworks is a construction company focused on outdoor structures such as: barns, woodsheds, greenhouses, and off-grid cabins. Our preference is to build using old world methods of timberframe and post 'n beam. Our structures will focus on usability and ease of maintenance. Each seeks to inspire a connection to the land, an appreciation for the outdoors, a love of family, a desire for the legacy of craftsmanship, and an acknowledgement of Creation.
